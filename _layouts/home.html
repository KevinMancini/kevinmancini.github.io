Website structure

Three sections, one consistent language.

The structure is intentionally simple: an academic page with curated data, and two journal-style sections for sport and art that can grow one post at a time.

Academy

Sport

Built like a clean journal so you can add one story and one photo set at a time.

Art

A second blog-style archive for essays, repertoire notes, and fragments.
